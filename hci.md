Enhancing Bimodal Communication with AI-Powered Glasses for English and ASL

Effective two-way communication between Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing (D/HoH) individuals and non-signers remains an ongoing challenge, particularly in dynamic, real-world scenarios. In this study, we evaluate a smart glasses-based communication system that leverages speech recognition and Large Language Models (LLMs) to support real-time ASL-English interactions.

IFF Interactive House
July 2024
Virtual Reality
Company: IFF

Tools
Unity Game Engine, Blender 3D Computer Graphics, C# Programming

The IFF Interactive House project is an interactive VR application for the IFF Sales team, working in collaboration with the Vice President of Sales, Marketing and Consumer Insights, to enhance marketing strategies. This application features a realistic log cabin house where users can interact with strategically placed IFF innovations. Upon interaction, a screen appears displaying product features, as well as teleportation areas, and the user is transported to a lounge area to watch an informative video on the Unique Selling Proposition (USP).
